1. Introduction

Welcome to Propeller AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered conversational platform.

By using Propeller AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our Service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture (via Google OAuth)
  • Chat Content: Messages, prompts, and conversations with our AI models
  • Avatar Questionnaires: Answers to personalization questions for specialized AI avatars
  • Uploaded Images: Images you upload for analysis (subject to usage limits)
  • Settings and Preferences: Language, theme, and other customization choices

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring URLs
  • Cookies and Local Storage: Session data, preferences, and authentication tokens

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process your requests, generate AI responses, and provide personalized experiences
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate access, and maintain security
  • Personalization: Customize AI avatar responses based on your questionnaire answers
  • Usage Limits: Track image uploads and enforce daily limits (2 images per 24 hours)
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Communication: Send service updates, security alerts, and respond to inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth for authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 AI Model Providers

We use third-party AI models to generate responses:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o): Used for image analysis and vision capabilities
  • DeepSeek: Used for text-based conversations

Your conversations and uploaded images are sent to these providers for processing. These providers have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4.3 Hosting and Infrastructure

Our Service is hosted on secure servers, and we use cloud infrastructure providers to store and process your data. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.

5. Data Storage and Retention

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in JSON files and local browser storage:

  • Server Storage: User profiles, chat history, avatar questionnaires, usage data
  • Browser Storage: Session data, preferences, cached data

5.2 Retention Period

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can delete your data at any time through the Settings page.

  • Chat History: Retained until you manually delete or until account deletion
  • Uploaded Images: Stored temporarily for processing, then removed after 24 hours
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and analytics

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
